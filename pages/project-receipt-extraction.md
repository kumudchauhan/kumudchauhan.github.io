      <div class="case-study-section">
        <h4>Approach</h4>
        <p>Designed an in-house ML system for key information extraction from digital receipts. Fine-tuned Transformer-based LLM models (BERT/DistilBERT) using PyTorch and Hugging Face. Built end-to-end training and inference pipelines on Amazon SageMaker with distributed training and real-time endpoints. Implemented human-in-the-loop evaluation and automated model retraining with corrected examples. Integrated experiment tracking with MLflow and Comet for reproducibility.</p>
      </div>

      <div class="case-study-section">
        <h4>Impact</h4>
